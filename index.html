Our History

Founded in 2013, the school was brought to life through the vision of Suthanya Hongkham and Wipawan Hongkham. Since its inception, the institution has been committed to providing exceptional language education, empowering students to succeed, and contributing positively to the local community.

We teach practical communication skills in Thai and other languages.
We help students speak, read and write confidently in real-life situations.
Our goal is to build students’ confidence so they can speak naturally and succeed in everyday conversations.
